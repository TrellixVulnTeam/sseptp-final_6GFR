Industry Institute Interaction Cell

Industry Institute Interaction cell acts as a bridge to share expertise, experience and infrastructure between Industry & Institute.

Functions

  • Facilitate Institute & Industry engagement through internships, joint projects with industry, seminars, workshops, Industrial visits, student exchange programs and placements.
  • Identify industry partners and proactively engage (tie-up) them to explore opportunities in technical research.
  • Facilitate SDP (Student Development Program) & FDP (Faculty Development Program) by inviting Industry Experts to give training on Technical & Soft skills
  • Act as a resource coordinator & provide mentor support to students working in Mega project teams, E-Cell and Student bodies.
  • Facilitate and organize activities of Atal Ranking (ARIIA) and Institution’s Innovation Council (IIC) - an initiative by MHRD
