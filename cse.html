Computer Science & Engineering Department (SOSO)

Enabling Computer Science & Engineering education through research and technological advancement to sustain with the growing demands of industries and society as a whole.

Mission

  • Creating Excellence in Computer Science & Engineering Through Academic Professionalism for the Changing Needs of the Society.
  • Establishing Centre of Excellence for Research and for Technical Development in The Area of Computer Science & Engineering.
  • Developing Communication Skill, Teamwork and Leadership Qualities for Continuing Education Among the Students, Through Project-Based and Team-Based Learning.
  • Inculcating Ethics and Human Values for Sustainable Societal Growth and Environmental Protection.
  • Empowering Students for Employability, Aspiring Higher Studies and to Become an entrepreneur.

Vision

  • Excellence in creating globally competent professionals and leaders in the field of computer science & engineering.

Research Areas

The faculty members and students of School of Computer Science do their research work and publishing their work in international and national Journals and conferences in the following areas such as GRID Computing, Big Data Analytics, Cloud Computing, Computer Security, WSN, Multimedia, Computer Vision and Image Processing, Computational Intelligence, Software engineering, Data Mining and Machine Learning etc. A number of studentSummer Internships are available.

The faculty and Student of the department involve in R & D and Industrial Consulting actively in the respective area of interest. A number of student Summer Internships are available in the department.

The Department of Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) is one of the highly preferred branch of study . The department administers bachelor’s programs in Computer Science and Engineering. It is monikered as SOSO.

The curriculum emphasizes on problem solving skills in C programming, b designing aspects, data structure and application level thinking through EBox Portal. Pattern recognition, data mining and machine learning, distributed systems and android application, operating systems and computer networks, computer security, and IOT computing.
