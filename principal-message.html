Principal Message

Dr. A. Senthil Kumar

Principal

B.E., M.E., MBA., PGDVLSI., DISM., Ph.D. (IITR)., PDF (TUT, SA).,

Senior PDF (VSB-TUO, EUROPE)., PGPAI&ML (University of Texas, Austin)

I am incredibly happy with the progress that we have been making to produce generations of well informed and ethics-oriented engineers. I am positive that in times to come we will continue this journey with elevated enthusiasm and persistently provide a platform of holistic learning to the young generation of learners.

We spare no efforts to make the educational experience of our students meaningful and relevant to the socio-economic needs of the times & to equip them to face the challenges of the future for leading the society from the front, while ensuring that at the same time they are enjoying their experience of learning.

My very competent team of faculty strive to focus on every student, monitor and mentor them, appreciate their achievement, and encourage them to overcome their shortcomings. I urge all the parents and faculty to create appropriate, meaningful, and participative learning situations, inculcating life skills, instilling confidence and hope to their students so that they are ready to not just survive but succeed in the world.
