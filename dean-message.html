Dear Parents and students,

Greetings from the Sanskrithi School of Engineering - Puttaparthi.

It gives me immense pleasure to welcome you to Sanskrithi School of Engineering. Choosing the right engineering program is one of the most important decisions that you will make in your life. You must be sure that you opt for the best and the latest. In an increasing global and competitive economic environment, innovation in line with market requirements is the key to ensuring professional success.

Keeping in mind the challenges and uncertainties, it is essential to select an engineering stream that aligns with your personal interest, future goals and financial ability. With SSE, you needn’t look further as we pride ourselves in providing the best faculty, infrastructure, international learning opportunities with transport and hostel and 100% placements assistance affordable to all. Our program is structured keeping in mind the constantly changing practices and standards in engineering fields. I take this opportunity to wish you a bright promising future and to guide you towards achieving your dreams and goals.

With best wishes,

Dr. BalaKoteswari M
