Mechanical Engineering(SMILE)

The world of Mechanical Engineering is constantly changing under the influence of new methods, advanced computational tools, improved simulation and analysis, and different manufacturing protocols. The SSE Mech Department (SMILE) organizes seminars, workshops and short-term training program in the emerging areas of Mechanical Engineering.

Vision

  • To train the students as professionals in Mechanical Engineering blended with leadership qualities to achieve excellence in the challenging future.

Mission

  • Maintaining excellence in Mechanical Engineering education through academic professionalism and teaching for the changing needs of the society.
  • Establishing Centre for research to promote industrial exposure in the area of Mechanical Engineering.
  • Developing communication skill, leadership qualities, teamwork and skills for continuing education among the students.
  • Inculcating ethics, human values for solving societal problems and environmental protection.
  • Creating opportunities to the students for experiencing real time problems through project-based learning to enhance employability and entrepreneurship.

Program Specific Outcome

  • Apply the knowledge of mathematics, science, engineering fundamentals, and Mechanical Engineering principles to the solution of complex problems in Mechanical Engineering.
  • Create, select, and apply appropriate techniques, resources, and modern engineering tools such as CAD, CAM, CIM and FEM including prediction and modelling to complex Mechanical Engineering activities with an understanding of the limitations.
  • Apply reasoning informed by the contextual knowledge to assess societal, health, safety, legal and cultural issues and the consequent responsibilities relevant to the professional Mechanical Engineering practice.
  • Environment and sustainability: Understand the impact of the professional Mechanical Engineering solutions in societal, environmental contexts, demonstrate the knowledge and need for sustainable development.

The faculty and Student of the department involve in R & D and Industrial Consulting actively in the respective area of interest. A number of student Summer Internships are available in the department.
