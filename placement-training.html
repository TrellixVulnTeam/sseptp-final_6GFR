Placements & Training

Vision & Mission

Vision

To provide for the best placements in line with student aspirations and solutions to enable excellence in academic performance and skills required according to industry standards.

Mission

To continuously work on improvements required in academics and non-academics for overall development of students as they get ready to step into the corporate/entrepreneur world.

Modus Operandi

  • To familiarize students to the latest technological developments in specific sectors through expert interface and interactions.
  • To bring in new skill developments through alignment with APSSDC programs.
  • To expose students to international languages – prepare for global representation.
  • Support languages like French, Spanish, German.
  • To equip and train students for International competitive examinations like GATE.
  • To invite special technical teams from international institutions to enable differentiated learning for students.
  • To invite international experts for imparting new teaching techniques followed internationally.
  • Organize inter-college student interface both within the campus and with other recognized national institutions.
  • To encourage interdisciplinary learning through interdepartmental interaction – understanding the commonality and interdependency between engineering departments.
  • Campus drives and online portals job applications.

Why Recruit from SSE

SSE prides itself in being one of the institutions in Andhra Pradesh in terms of attracting the remote rural students and placing them onto the mainstream corporations. Special attention is given to understand the skill gaps in students and provide unique platforms of learning to build confidence and character in students.

There is a plethora of opportunities for students graduating out from the portals of various educational institutions. But the process of seizing the right job is manifold, be it for the corporate world or industrial establishments. However, the fact is that there is tough competition in the job market when comes to matching the skills available for a job profile. This is where the placement cell comes into action. It eases the process of recruitment for both companies and students. With the help of Placements Cell, students can put forth their abilities and knowledge in front of recruiters to get hold of the best of jobs. Hitherto, it takes the right efforts, and qualifications to sustain as well as grow in the career space

Some of the focused areas to inculcate skills in students are as follows

  • Business communications
  • Critical appreciation of current issues
  • Aptitude tests
  • Coding Skills
  • Embedding soft skill courses in the curriculum
  • Alumni, industry experts speak
  • Career grooming sessions
  • Imbibing critical thinking
  • Interaction with entrepreneurs
  • Entrepreneurial courses
  • Mock GDs and Presentations
  • Mentoring sessions
  • Creating entrepreneurs
  • Encouraging research
  • Assisting in Higher Education

SSE Placements Team

SSE Placements Cell ensures that the students are in sync with evolving market needs and prepare them for converting campus placement opportunities into job offers. SSE Placements Cell play’s a key role in defining the recruiters’ ideal candidate. Not only are the theories taught, but seminars, assignments, micro-projects, major projects, curriculum, pedagogy play a part in the overall development of students. We encourage students to discover their purpose and are provided with skills and training to enable them to be job ready.
