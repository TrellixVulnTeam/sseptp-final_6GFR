Recognition & Affiliation

Sanskrithi School of Engineering has entered into Memoranda of Understanding with many corporate and industrial organizations to strengthen the skill sets it provides its students. Ranging from educational material to soft skills that improve the employability of the students, these MOUs will help SSE (to) create character-shaping and life changing training modules for students, helping them garner more success in life – as employees and as human beings.

RISE, Austria

This Memorandum of Understanding (“MOU”) outlines the intentions of RISE, VIENNA, Austria, and Sanskrithi School of Engineering regarding the establishment of a long–term cooperative endeavor intended to create mutually advantageous relationships in academics.

Focus of the MOU

  • RISE will support to establish an R & D lab in SSE and maintained by SSE within the prescribed framework.
  • RISE and SSE facilitate the students under exchange program where SSE students get an opportunity to visit Vienna for technology exchange
  • RISE will depute two faculties to SSE on a regular basis to teach various engineering subjects and Value-added courses
  • RISE and SSE work together to make SSE an integral part of RISE in the long run.
  • RISE and SSE work together to organize international conference, FDPs, Hackathons etc.

IAESTE, India

This Memorandum of Association (MOA) is made between IAESTE-India and Sanskrithi Group of Institutions, Sanskrithi School of Engineering

  • To associate with IAESTE-India through the student exchange program of IAESTE by providing internships to foreign students.
  • Promoting international understanding through exchanging of students worldwide

APSSDC

APSSDC is a unique organization formed as a public private partnership (PPP) corporation to promote skill-development & entrepreneurship in the state of Andhra Pradesh. Our MOU with them will allow us to give our students an opportunity to learn the career skills that will help them succeed.

Focus of the MOU

  • To make qualitative improvements in imparting technical skills by setting up- CM skill Excellence Centre
  • To promote industries
  • Train student to improve employability
  • Promote Research and Development and innovation for existing industries
