Alankrithi

From dramatics to literature, from poetry to music and dance, this is the place to come and experience it all. This club hosts a variety of enjoyable activities that not only entertain but also an opportunity to students to explore and discover their creative and innovation skills.

Speakers Club

Speakers' Club is a forum for students to improve their public speaking skills. The group provides students with an opportunity to practice their speaking and language skills. The Speakers Club will assist you with getting familiar with English as a LARW skill, overcoming language fear, build confidence to communicate and give you constructive criticism on how to improve. You could also meet mentors who can help you in both personal and academic life

Coders Club

Coding Club aims to establish a coding culture on campus, reaching every student passionate about coding. The club’s motto is to Code-Create-Innovate. The club arranges weekly workshops focusing on various activities such as, basic programming, web designing languages, machine learning algorithms, and mobile application development. Programming competitions are a frequent feature of this Club.

Hruday- Coffee with Acharya

Coffee with Acharya is a platform where members of the club get to interact with field experts in an informal setting. Field experts get a friendly environment to interact with students and share with them their life experiences.

Sports Club

The Sports Club makes it possible for students to engage in a variety of sports and games. and tournaments hosted by various institutions. Intra-college tournaments in a variety of sports are conducted to keep students physically fit and motivated while keeping their mind off the strenuous academic schedule.
