Research Industrial Systems Engineering Enterprises – RISE, Austria.

The Research Industrial Systems Engineering Enterprises is an International IT Research, Software, System Development. Consulting and Service Company who operates with many universities in Europe and Austria, with a special connection to the Vienna University of Technology and Modul University of Vienna. RISE emerged as a spinoff from the Vienna University of Technology and provides extraordinary concentrated global “people ware” in information technology and systems engineering for more than 25 years.
