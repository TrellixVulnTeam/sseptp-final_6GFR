Electrical & Electronics Engineering (PPP)

Vision

  • To transform the students as leaders in Electrical & Electronics Engineering to achieve professional excellence in the challenging world.

Mission

  • To create an environment for the students to have strong academic fundamentals and enable them to be life-long learners.
  • To provide modern tools to the students in the field of Electrical & Electronics to meet the real-world challenges.
  • To Develop Communication skill, leadership qualities, teamwork and skills for continuing education among the students.
  • To inculcate Ethics, Human values for solving societal problems and environmental protection.
  • Validate engineering knowledge through innovative research projects to enhance their employability and entrepreneurship skills.

Program Specific Outcome

  • Graduate will be able to identify, analyze & solve the problems related to Electrical & Electronics Engineering by applying the fundamental knowledge of Electrical & Electronics.
  • Graduate will demonstrate an ability to investigate, design and develop both software and hardware using significant knowledge of modern tools in Electrical & Electronics Engineering
  • Graduate will be able to apply their knowledge to assess societal, environmental, health, safety issues with professional ethics and can also pursue higher studies, involve in research activities, be employable or entrepreneur.
